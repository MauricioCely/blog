I like to analyze data to answer research questions and test hypotheses. Currently I investigate questions related to the turbulence in the stable Atmospheric Boundary Layer through my work as a PhD in Physics student at [Universidade Federal de Santa Maria](https://www.ufsm.br/cursos/pos-graduacao/santa-maria/pgfisica/) in the Department of Physics.

I graduated as Physics teacher from the [Universidad Distrital Francisco Jose de Caldas](https://www.udistrital.edu.co/) based in Bogotá, Colombia. Then I traveled to Brazil to start with a MS in Physics in 2016. In 2018 I began working on my PhD in Physics, where I am investigating statistical methods to parameterize the Atmospheric Boundary Layer under the advisement of [Dr. Luca Mortarini](https://www.researchgate.net/profile/Luca_Mortarini) at [Italian National Research Council](https://www.cnr.it/en). I expect to graduate by the end of 2022.

I am a R enthusiast. I enjoy [programming](/tag/rstats/) and [photographing](#gallery).
